Google Cloud AI

Why this only works with Vertex AI + GCS

Remove Google Cloud AI and the system becomes a static form. Here is what it actually provides:

🧠 Language Intelligence

Gemini turns rough bullet points into polished, role-specific JD prose that matches Globe's hiring standards — no template library can replicate this.

📂 Reference Grounding

Reference documents stored in GCS are injected into every prompt, keeping JD tone, terminology, and structure on-brand automatically.

⚡ Speed & Iteration

Multi-turn chat lets hiring managers refine a JD in real time. What used to take half a day now takes under 5 minutes.

🏢 Enterprise Deployment Path

Vertex AI integrates with GCP IAM, VPC controls, and regional data residency — making the demo production-ready with minimal changes.

💡

Key takeaway: Google Cloud AI is the core differentiator — it provides context-aware language generation that is grounded in company data, fast to iterate, and built for enterprise scale.
